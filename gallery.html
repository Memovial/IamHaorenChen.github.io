My Hometown

Taishan, my small hometown in Guangdong, is more than just a place; it's the embodiment of home. It is a small village, the tight-knit community and familiar surroundings made every corner feel like a warm embrace. I've been away for years, and the yearning for the simplicity and genuine connections of Taishan pulls at my heart. Memories of shared laughter, the scent of street-side snacks, and the tranquil beauty of the countryside make me miss it deeply. Taishan isn't just where I'm from; it's where I belong, and the distance only amplifies my love for this little haven that I can't wait to revisit.
